# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Truck drivers and dispatchers use this app while planning, reviewing, and revisiting compliant trips. They need to see their recent work quickly and begin a new plan without navigating through visual noise.

## Product Purpose

TruckDispatch helps users plan routes, generate compliant ELD daily logs, and retain trip history. The dashboard is the practical starting point for these workflows.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, dependable. The interface should feel organized and approachable, with the confidence of an operational tool rather than the density of a back-office system.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic metric-card grids, decorative dashboard chrome, overly saturated operational colors, and course-dashboard content patterns copied without a dispatch purpose.

## Design Principles

1. Put the next useful action in clear view.
2. Make trip information scannable before it is detailed.
3. Use emphasis sparingly so operational status remains legible.
4. Preserve familiar controls and workflows.
5. Reflow layouts structurally for small screens, never by shrinking desktop UI.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Preserve visible focus states, semantic controls, readable touch targets, and a responsive layout that works with text zoom and narrow screens.
